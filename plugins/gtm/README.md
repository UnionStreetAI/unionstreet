# GTM Plugin

GTM capability plugin for marketing, growth, sales, and revenue operations
agents. It vendors the MIT-licensed `coreyhaines31/marketingskills` skill set
so a Union Street GTM department can receive one `gtm` plugin grant and get a
full tactical marketing skill graph.

## Included Skill Areas

- Product marketing context and positioning.
- CRO for pages, signup, onboarding, forms, popups, and paywalls.
- Copywriting, editing, cold email, email sequences, social, image, and video.
- SEO, AI SEO, schema, site architecture, programmatic SEO, and directories.
- Paid ads, ad creative, analytics tracking, A/B testing, and measurement.
- Launch strategy, pricing, RevOps, sales enablement, customer research,
  competitor profiling, churn prevention, referrals, community, and lead magnets.
